Dietitians

Registered dietitians have specialized
nutritional and medical training that helps
them determine a person's dietary needs and
plan the person's meals in and out of the hospital,
as requested by the attending physician.

Maternity Care

Maternity wards are equipped with midwife
teams, obstetricians, and other healthcare
professionals who help women through
pregnancy, labor, and delivery.

Emergency
Department Services

Emergency departments are equipped with medical specialists, staff, and equipment to respond to traumatic injuries and medical emergencies, such as heart attacks and strokes.

          

          

Diagnostic services

We offer a range of diagnostic services,
including laboratory testing, X-rays, ultrasounds,
and other imaging studies.

Telemedicine

We can also provide medical care and advice
to patients remotely, through the use of digital
technology, such as video conferencing,
telephone, or online chats.

Medical and
Surgical Care

This is the main service offered by our hospital and consists of a comprehensive range of services including emergency services, in-patient care, and surgery.
